Abyssal Heart

Award: Best Art - GDS Welcome Jam 2025
Role: Programmer & Project Coordinator
Tech: C#, Unity, GitHub

Play on Itch.io

The title orb concept represents the "heart" artifact.

The Technical Challenge

The core challenge was implementing a Global State-Switching Mechanic within 24 hours. We engineered an event-driven system where bioluminescence state changes affect "Light Listeners" across the environment.

Sanity Mechanic: As the player loses sanity, environmental objects shift—friendly NPCs can become aggressive, and controls become more fluid/challenging to simulate deep-sea swimming.

Biomimetic Design

Protagonist: Ef

Based on the Firefly Squid, mixing humanoid elements with aquatic biology.

The Deep-Sea Cast

NPCs like Ernie, Hyde, and Lord Inklord IV were designed to populate three distinct stages of increasing depth.

Project Coordination & Reflection

Coordination Wins

Despite the 24-hour constraint, the team remained highly communicative, successfully delivering a working prototype within the timeframe.

Lessons Learned

  • Structure: We identified a need for better folder structure and naming conventions for smoother GitHub repository management.
  • Scope: Future development will focus on a Final Boss (Bullet Hell stage) and improved narrative cutscenes.

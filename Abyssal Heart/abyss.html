The Technical Challenge

The core challenge was implementing a Global State-Switching Mechanic within 24-hours. The team engineered an event-driven system where the light switch (bioluminescence) state changes across all environmental "Light Listeners," allowing for complex puzzle logic. This would then allow the character to navigate around obstacles e.g. walls and other environmental changes effected by the light surrounding the character. The sanity mechanic would also trigger other objects which may effect the environment around the character such as friendly npcs suddenly becomming agressive. Light is therefore a mechanic that both influences the state of the environment and creates challenges for the player.

Project Synopsis

After being exiled by the Queen, a lost knight finds themselves on a quest to retrieve the artifact that caused this spiral to begin with. Using bioluminescence, switch between the light and the dark to solve puzzles, interact with NPCS, and reach the deepest trenches of the ocean.

Ef the Firefly

Within this game, we play as a character named Firefly (Ef), who is based on a Firefly Squid. Every character is based on different sea creatures, mixing humanoid elements with those of said creatures.

About Mark

Games Developer

Since the age of 10, I have always had a keen interest in Software, Games and Design: my first exposure to Game Development was modding a game called Little Fighter 2 where I made sprites and created my own character, from there I moved onto RPG Toolkit where I learned how to make RPG games. Nowadays I develop games using GameMaker, Unity, Blender and Unreal Engine.

My greatest Inspiration has always been Hideo Kojima his Metal Gear Solid Games has been a source of inspiration when it came to story-telling and UI Design. His unique character designs and thrilling gameplay has since carried over to his Death Stranding Games. If I ever get to meet him in person, I wish to shake his hand and thank him for his dedication to his craft.

My hobbies and interests include: Lacrosse, Cooking, Photography, Movies, Creative Writing, Dungeons and Dragons, Board Games, Scuba Diving, Motorcycles, Art, Software Development and Robotics.
